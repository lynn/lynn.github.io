Spritskoekjes

Translated from Het grote boek met de heerlijkste bakrecepten (Teubner, 1983).

Ingredients

  • 300g of butter;
  • 250g of brown sugar;
  • 125g of corn starch;
  • 225ml of milk;
  • a pinch of salt;
  • zest of one lemon;
  • 500g of flour, sifted;
  • 100g of chocolate (for melting / dipping);
  • for the baking trays: butter or margarine.
You'll need a star-nozzled piping bag, too.

Recipe

  1. Grease one or two baking trays with butter or margarine. [In my experience, you'll need two.]
  2. Mix the butter, brown sugar, and cornstarch into a smooth, non-foamy, mixture.
  3. For now, add only 150ml of milk.
  4. Mix in the salt, lemon zest, and finally sifted flour. Knead the dough.
  5. Only add the last bit of milk if the dough is too thick for the piping bag.
  6. Preheat the oven to 190 Â°C.
  7. Put the dough in the piping bag, and pipe S-shapes and circles onto the trays.
  8. Bake for 10 minutes in the middle of the oven until golden brown.
  9. Melt the chocolate in a water bath. [I just used the microwave in 15-second increments.]
  10. Once cooled down, dip the cookies in the chocolate and let dry.
[This makes like 20 cookies.]
