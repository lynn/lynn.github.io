I’m Lynn a.k.a. @chordbug! I’m a programmer and musician based in Antwerp, Belgium. I’m trans, and my pronoun is she.

I like math, linguistics, old sound chips, interactive fiction, retro computers, typography, and cute anime art. Sometimes I dabble in drawing or writing.

As a programmer, I like to daydream about accessibility, localization, Unicode, type systems, monads, UX design, and the <ruby> (yeah!) tag. At times, I get really into code golf. My less pithy code is on GitHub.

I’m spending a lot of my free time now studying Japanese. I have a SoundCloud, but more of my musical output exists as sketches posted to Twitter.

Oh: I made koakuma, a Discord trivia bot that has you guess the common tag shared by 9 random pieces of anime-style artwork. Come play!

If you like what I do, you can buy me a coffee. ♡

Thanks for stopping by.
