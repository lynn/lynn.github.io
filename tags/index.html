Summary of some confusable HTML tags

(namely, the ones that mark up inline text in a bunch of ways)

Tag name Use case Code sample Output
b Draw attention to words without conveying extra importance. A <b>golden yo-yo</b> rests on the <b>trophy case</b>. A golden yo-yo rests on the trophy case.
strong Importance, seriousness, urgency. <strong>Warning: contains live bees.</strong> Warning: contains live bees.
i An alternate voice or mood; a different quality of text, such as a technical term, a transliteration, a thought, a ship name… I'm allergic to cats (<i>Felis catus</i>)… Well, <i>c'est la vie</i>. I'm allergic to cats (Felis catus)… Well, c'est la vie.
em Contrastive stress emphasis, as in spoken language. I didn't say <em>she</em> stole my Etruscan boar vessel. I didn't say she stole my Etruscan boar vessel.
cite The title of a work Haven't you read <cite>Infinite Jest</cite>?<br>…Yeah, me neither. Haven't you Infinite Jest?
…Yeah, me neither.
s Content that is no longer accurate or relevant. <s>Price: $19.99.</s> Now only $29.99! Price: $19.99. Now only $29.99!
del A deletion to a document. Telekinesis is <del>cool</del> <ins>cute</ins>. <button>Save this edit</button> Telekinesis is cool cute.
u Other unarticulated annotation. (Example: of a grammar construct.) Ana reported <u>who was at the party</u> and <u>how long they stayed</u>. Ana reported who was at the party and how long they stayed.
small Disclaimers, caveats, semantically fine print. 15% off on your next purchase! <small>Coupons cannot be stacked (except physically).</small> 15% off on your next purchase! Coupons cannot be stacked (except physically).
mark Marked for reference, due to relevance in another context. (Example: search results.) 3 results: eff<mark>ici</mark>ent, elast<mark>ici</mark>ty, med<mark>ici</mark>ne 3 results: efficient, elasticity, medicine
